## Discovery boundaries

- Included: official ULS pages and official ULS-hosted PDFs only.
- Included program scope: MASTER and PHD/doctorate-level evidence.
- Excluded: Bachelor, diplomas, certificates, continuing education, training, professional programs outside degree listings, news, and events.
- External partner/application links were noted only when referenced from official ULS pages, but were not added as sources because the requirement is official ULS pages/PDFs only.
