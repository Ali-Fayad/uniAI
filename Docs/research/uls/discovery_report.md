## Scope

This is a discovery-only pass for official Université La Sagesse sources. Only MASTER and PHD/doctorate-level evidence is in scope. Bachelor, diplomas, certificates, continuing education, training, professional programs outside official degree tables, news, and events are excluded.

## Executive summary

ULS publishes a consolidated official Academics page that lists graduate programs by faculty. Official master-level evidence was found in Law, Economics and Business Administration, Political Science and International Relations, Public Health, Tourism and Hotel Management, Religious and Theological Sciences, and Canon Law. Official doctoral-level evidence was found for a Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA) offered by ULS in partnership with ISC Paris.

The official sources inspected did not show a conventional PhD program list. Doctoral evidence found is DBA-specific. No official PhD evidence found.

Graduate tuition is publicly available from official ULS sources on the Tuition Fees page for 2025-2026, with graduate credit pricing and related fees by faculty.

  - Dual/double degree from Université La Sagesse and Université de Bordeaux, France.
  - Credits: 42.
- Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA)
