## Discovery notes

- The official graduate application instructions are the strongest single source for the graduate program inventory because they list graduate-level majors grouped by faculty.
- Program pages were found for representative/major graduate offerings under Arts and Sciences, Business and Economics, Engineering, and Health Sciences.
- The official public site has dynamic/truncated pages in browser extraction. Search snippets and direct official URLs were used to supplement where page-open extraction returned only image placeholders.
- No official AUST PhD/doctoral program page was found during this discovery pass.
