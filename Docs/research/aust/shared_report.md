## Admissions Summary

- Graduate applicants use the official online graduate application.
- Required documents include CV, ID or passport copy, passport photos, application fee, family civil-status record where applicable, certified certificates, transcript, and recommendations.
- Current graduate admission requirements confirm bachelor's-or-equivalent standing and an English-proficiency requirement.

## Scholarship Summary

- The official financial-aid page confirms partial scholarships for candidates with financial need.
- Aid remains conditional on the page's academic and registration criteria.

## Financial Aid Summary

- Need-based partial aid is available.
- The page also ties aid to completed credits, full-time registration, and GPA conditions.

## Payment Summary

- Students pay 25% of tuition plus other fees at the beginning of each semester.
- Deferred payment is available by signed contract.
- Installments are payable at the bank.

## Graduate Regulations

- Master's degrees require 30 to 39 credits depending on program.
- Minimum cumulative GPA is 3.00/4.00.
- No more than two C grades are allowed.
- The rules PDF notes a minimum 6-credit load for full-time graduate status.
