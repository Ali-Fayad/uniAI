## Admissions findings

Source coverage: `AOU_SRC_003`, `AOU_SRC_005`, `AOU_SRC_006`, `AOU_SRC_007`

### MBA requirements

- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution or equivalent degree evaluated by the Ministry of Higher Education.
- Relevant professional experience.
- Minimum undergraduate GPA of 2.5 appears on the Apply - Graduate and MBA detail pages.
- English placement test required unless exempted by valid language evidence.
- Graduate Entrance Exam (GEE) required unless exempted by GMAT/equivalent evidence.
- Non-business background applicants must complete 16 credit hours of remedial courses.

### MSc Computing requirements

- BSc in Information Technology-related subjects from AOU or another recognized university.
- Apply page specifies minimum GPA of 2.5.
- Applicants from mathematics, physics, business computing, or other relevant backgrounds may need 12 additional credit-courses from the BSc ITC program before starting the MSc.
- English placement test required unless exempted by valid language evidence.

### MA TEFL requirements

- BA degree in English Language and Literature or related specialization from AOU or another recognized university.
- Graduation rating of GOOD or higher as evaluated by the Ministry of Higher Education.
- IELTS Academic band 6 or higher, or TOEFL paper-based 550 or equivalent computer-based score.

### Required documents for graduate admission

- Application form
- Academic credentials:
  - Certified copy of Lebanese equivalency of bachelor's degree
  - Official transcript
- Professional credentials / employment certificates
- Updated CV or resume
- Two recommendation letters
- Copy of valid national ID

## Fees / tuition / payment findings

Source coverage: `AOU_SRC_004`, `AOU_SRC_006`, `AOU_SRC_009`, `AOU_SRC_019`

Graduate admission fee items found:

- Application fee: USD 50
- Registration fee: USD 200
- English placement test for MBA applicants: USD 30
- MBA GEE test fee: USD 30

MBA General detail page tuition and additional fees found:

- Mandatory courses: 36 credits at USD 170/credit
- Elective courses: 12 credits at USD 170/credit
- Total credits: 48
- Operational fee: USD 100 per semester
- NSSF: 8,400,000 LBP paid annually
- Registration fee: USD 200
- NSSF exemption notes:
  - Students above 30 years old are exempted.
  - Students covered under parents' NSSF must provide official proof.

Payment and refund notes:

- New and continuing students may pay tuition in installments.
- Graduate FAQ says tuition can be divided into 4 payments during each semester.
- Program detail refund schedule:
  - Before semester start / during add-drop: 100% refund
  - After add-drop: 50% refund
  - After third week: 0% refund
