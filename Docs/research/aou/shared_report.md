## Admissions Summary

- Graduate applicants apply through the official graduate application and submit the required supporting documents.
- MBA and MSc applicants are subject to English placement testing unless exempted.
- MBA applicants are also subject to the Graduate Entrance Exam unless exempted by GMAT or equivalent evidence.
- The graduate requirements page confirms degree-equivalency review by the Ministry of Higher Education.

## Required Documents Summary

- Completed graduate application
- Degree equivalency or Ministry evaluation where applicable
- Official transcript
- Professional/employment certificates
- Updated CV or resume
- Two recommendation letters
- Valid national ID copy

## Language Requirements Summary

- MBA and MSc applicants: English placement test unless exempted.
- Exemptions include IELTS 7.0, paper TOEFL 600, TOEFL CBT 250, or TOEFL iBT 100 within 2 years.
- MA TEFL: IELTS Academic 6 or TOEFL paper-based 550 or equivalent CBT score.
