## Graduate Admissions / Required Documents

Source: `GU-SRC-003`

Graduate admission requirements include:
- Filled application form
- Certified copy of Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent
- Bachelor's degree from a recognized institution of higher learning
- Certified copy of bachelor's degree equivalence
- Official transcript of undergraduate courses
- Recent passport-size photo
- Lebanese applicants: passport or national identity card, family civil status record, NSSF declaration form for students under 30
- Non-Lebanese applicants: passport copy and valid residence permit copy
- Non-refundable 50 USD application fee

## Tuition / Fees / Payment Plan

Source: `GU-SRC-010`

2025-2026 tuition rows relevant to graduate scope:
- Masters of Arabic Literature: 170 USD/credit
- Masters of Education: 170 USD/credit
- Masters of Business Administration: 170 USD/credit
- PHD Arabic: 200 USD/credit — note only; not accepted as official PhD program evidence

Other relevant fee/payment evidence:
- Application Fee: 50 USD once
- Registration Fee: 75 USD in Summer; 150 USD in Fall & Spring
- First Payment: 500 USD in Fall & Spring; 50% of total fees in Summer
- Students who want installments fill a deferred payment form, and accepted students sign a deferred payment contract.
- Payment schedule shown for Fall, Spring, and Summer.
- Deposit: accepted students pay 20% of semester tuition to reserve a seat; non-refundable.
- Refund policy says Graduate and Special Students have no refund available.
