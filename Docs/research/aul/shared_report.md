# AUL Shared Data Report

## Shared Admissions Summary

AUL's admissions workflow requires applicants to complete the Admission Application Form at the campus of registration and submit the required documents by the deadlines assigned by Admissions.

## Required Documents Summary

AUL's official admissions page lists the following graduate-facing documents:

- Admission Application Form (4 pages)
- Three recent passport-size colored photos
- Certified copy of Identity Card or Passport
- Certified copy of Family Register
- Certified copy of Official Lebanese BAC II Certificate or equivalent / freshman authorization with SAT
- Certified copy of high-school grades for the last three years
- Certified copies of degree(s) and official transcript(s) from previous university stamped by the Lebanese Ministry of Education & Higher Education, with equivalence for graduate programs
- International testing scores and certificates, if available
- Medical report
- NSSF letter, if available
- Proof of residency letter
