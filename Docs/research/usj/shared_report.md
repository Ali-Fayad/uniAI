## Tuition model

USJ does not expose a centralized public graduate tuition table in the official pages reviewed here.

What is confirmed:

- Graduate study is organized on the ECTS system.
- One credit equals 25 hours of work.
- A master's cycle is generally 90 to 120 credits.
- A doctorate cycle is 180 credits.
- Full-time status is 24 credits or more per semester.
- Part-time status is 15 credits or more per semester.
- The university can allow payment to be spread over several university months without fees.

What is not confirmed:

- Central graduate tuition schedule
- Program/faculty tuition amounts
- Application fee
- Registration fee
- Technology fee
- Student activity fee
- Lab fee
- Thesis fee
- Graduation fee

## Shared admissions

Confirmed shared graduate admission surface:

- Master's admissions page exists.
- Doctorate admissions page exists.
- Graduate admissions depend on the applicant's prior university title and the chosen program.
- Some programs can require an interview, file review, or aptitude test.
- Prior degrees are subject to equivalence review.
- Foreign and most non-Lebanese prior diplomas require recognition/equivalence.
- USJ welcomes applicants of all nationalities.

## Shared documents

Confirmed:

- A dedicated graduate documents page exists.
- Graduate admission is handled through an application file.

Not fully extracted in this pass:

- The complete checklist of required documents

## Shared language requirements

Confirmed:

- USJ states that the university's teaching languages are French and Arabic.
- Some programs are taught in English.
- The regulations require master's students to validate at least two Arabic credits.
- Some institutions may extend French aptitude requirements to graduate cycles.

Not confirmed:

- A single university-wide graduate English test threshold

## Payment summary

Confirmed:

- Payment can be spread over several university months without fees.
- USJ loans are interest-free.
- Repayment begins one year after the planned diploma date.

Not confirmed:

- A separate published graduate installment plan

## Accreditation and grading

Confirmed:

- USJ states institutional reaccreditation by ACQUIN for 8 years in September 2025.
- USJ uses ECTS.
- A credit equals 25 hours of work.
- A student is full-time at 24 credits or more per semester.
- A student is part-time at 15 credits or more per semester.
- A degree generally requires at least 50% of its credits to be completed at USJ.
