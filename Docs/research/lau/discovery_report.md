# LAU Graduate Source Discovery Report

Date accessed: 2026-06-26

Captured source inventory: 53 unique official LAU URLs.

## Verdict

Partial.

## What Was Confirmed

- LAU does offer graduate programs.
- The official graduate listing is the [Graduate Programs](https://www.lau.edu.lb/study/graduate.php) page.
- LAU has an official graduate studies / research hub at [Graduate Studies and Research](https://gsr.lau.edu.lb/).
- LAU has official source surfaces for:
  - schools/faculties
  - program listings
  - admissions
  - tuition / fees
  - financial aid
  - scholarships
  - calendar / deadlines
  - catalog / academic rules

## Key Findings

- The graduate listing page surfaces master’s programs, online master’s programs, the MD program, and the PharmD program.
- The listing also mentions graduate certificates through the online-programs section, but a dedicated public graduate-certificate landing page has not yet been confirmed.
- A dedicated public graduate PhD listing page was not found in the source reconnaissance pass.
- School and program pages were located for:
  - School of Architecture and Design
  - School of Arts and Sciences
  - Adnan Kassar School of Business
  - School of Engineering
  - School of Medicine
  - School of Nursing
  - School of Pharmacy
