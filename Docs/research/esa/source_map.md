Task code: ESA_DISCOVERY_001_BROWSER_INV  
University: École Supérieure des Affaires (ESA Business School)  
Official website: https://www.esa.edu.lb  
Discovery date: 2026-07-07

## Source selection rules

Only official ESA URLs were used. Pages from `esa.edu.lb` are treated as official. The `info.esa.edu.lb` brochure-gate pages appeared in search results but were not used in `sources.json` because the validation requirement asked for official ESA URLs only and the primary official program pages already provided sufficient evidence.

Out-of-scope material excluded: executive education pages that do not directly describe a Master/DBA degree, certificates, short courses, professional training, news, events, alumni pages, faculty profiles, partnerships without degree evidence, and advertisements.

## Notes and exclusions

- Bachelor in Business Administration (BBA) is visible on ESA program pages but excluded from findings because it is undergraduate.
- Certificates and executive education entries such as CDD, CFA, TEF, shipping/logistics, digital marketing, arts management, and banking qualifications are excluded unless they are degree programs.
- ESA Santé overview and health expertise pages mention health-management training but are not primary evidence because the MEMS degree page is the stronger official source.
- Events/news/testimonials were ignored.
