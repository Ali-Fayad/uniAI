## Master's evidence

Official Master's evidence found.

The official Master's requirements page is titled `متطلبات التسجيل لمرحلة الماجستير`. It states that BIU created a specialization stage after the license level and that graduates of the Faculty of Sharia may continue in specializations. The page lists these Master's/specialization areas:

1. `شعبة أصول الفقه` / Usul al-Fiqh
2. `شعبة التخصص في الفقه المقارن` / Comparative Fiqh
3. `شعبة الدراسات الإسلامية` / Islamic Studies

The same page states that the specialization-stage study lasts one academic year, followed by a scientific thesis for at least one full year and at most two years by supervisor request and approval.

The official admissions page separately lists required documents for `مرحلة الماجستير` and `مرحلة الماجستير تحضيرية`.

The official course-description index contains a `الدراسات العليا` section with:

- `شعبة الدراسات الإسلامية`
- `شعبة الفقه المقارن`
- `ماجستير في القضاء الشرعي`
- `السنة التحضيرية للماجستير شعبة الدراسات الإسلامية`
- `السنة التحضيرية للماجستير شعبة الحقوق`

## PhD / Doctorate evidence

Official PhD evidence found.

The official Doctorate requirements page is titled `متطلبات التسجيل مرحلة العالمية الدكتوراه`. It states that the Master's specialization is not sufficient for those undertaking social/religious reform work and that continued study is needed for `العالمية الدكتوراه`.

The page states:

- The student prepares the dissertation over three years.
- The student must publish at least two research papers in a refereed/documented/indexed journal or international conference in the specialization, based on a Ministry of Education and Higher Education equivalence committee decision.
- Admission to the three doctorate specializations is limited to holders of the Master's specialization in the same field.

The page names the three doctorate specializations as:

1. `الفقه المقارن` / Comparative Fiqh
2. `أصول الفقه` / Usul al-Fiqh
3. `الدراسات الإسلامية` / Islamic Studies

The official admission requirements page also lists required documents for `مرحلة الدكتوراه`, including the Master's specialization degree and a printed/corrected copy of the Master's thesis.

## Admissions / required documents

The official admission requirements page states that all stages require a recommendation certificate from two sheikhs. It then lists stage-specific required documents.

For Master's:

- Individual civil registry record
- Secondary certificate or official equivalent
- University high license degree with university transcript
- Four certified personal photos
- Health certificate
- Criminal record

For preparatory Master's:

- Individual civil registry record
- Secondary certificate or official equivalent
- University high license degree with university transcript
- Four certified personal photos
- Health certificate
- Criminal record

For Doctorate:

- Individual civil registry record
- Secondary certificate or official equivalent
- University high license degree with university transcript
- Four certified personal photos
- Health certificate
- Criminal record
- Master's specialization degree with printed and corrected copy of the thesis
