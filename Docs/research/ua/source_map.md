## Scope

In scope: Master, PhD / Doctorate evidence.  
Out of scope: Bachelor, Licence, Diplomas, Certificates, Continuing education, Training, Professional programs, News, Events.

News/events were not used as program sources. Event/news-like pages were retained only where they helped evaluate whether UA has doctoral/PhD activity outside the normal graduate program inventory.

## PhD / Doctorate source findings

No official UA page in the graduate program inventory lists a PhD / Doctor of Philosophy / doctoral degree program as a normal UA graduate program. Official pages do show doctoral co-supervision, doctoral scholarships, and PhD-student research mobility contexts. These are recorded as evidence that doctoral-related activity exists around UA research/international partnerships, but they are not sufficient to extract UA PhD programs.

## Browser limitations

- Some UA pages use dynamic loading and repeated menu content; extracted browser text includes noise such as repeated menus and `Loading...` placeholders.
- The repository root was not available in the execution environment. Files were created under `/mnt/data/research/ua` instead.
- The browser/web tool could inspect official pages/PDF text. Direct network access from the container was unavailable, so validation was limited to local JSON/schema checks and known official URL host checks.
