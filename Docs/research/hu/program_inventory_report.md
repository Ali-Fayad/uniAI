## Grouped vs Standalone Decisions

- The six MBA rows published by HU in the graduate index were normalized into one MBA record.
- This follows the discovery guidance that the MBA PDFs share a common degree framework and specialization areas, and that concentrations should be modeled as concentrations rather than separate programs unless HU clearly treats them as distinct degrees.

## MBA Concentration Decision

- HU's official MBA materials expose a shared MBA framework with specialization areas.
- The inventory models the MBA as one program with concentrations:
  - General Business Administration
  - Accounting
  - Finance
  - Human Resources Management
  - Management
  - Marketing
- This is the most conservative interpretation available from the official sources.
