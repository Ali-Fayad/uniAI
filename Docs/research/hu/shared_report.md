# HU Shared Data Report

## Shared Admissions Summary

HU's graduate admissions flow is centralized around the Office of Orientation and Admissions and faculty-level review. Applicants submit the graduate application file, meet faculty-specific criteria, and complete any requested interview or entrance-exam steps.

## Required Documents Summary

The official catalog and admissions pages support a graduate checklist that includes:

- Completed graduate application file
- Passport-size photo
- Civil ID copy for Lebanese applicants
- Passport copy for non-Lebanese applicants
- Certified bachelor's degree or equivalent
- Official undergraduate transcript
- Other faculty-specific supporting documents if requested

## Language Requirement Summary

HU uses English as the language of instruction.

- MBA applicants: TOEFL 550 or equivalent
- MA applicants: TOEFL 600 or equivalent

The catalog also refers to alternative standardized English test pathways.

## Deadline Summary

HU publishes annual admissions timing and calendar guidance. The catalog shows the following graduate application windows:

- Fall application deadline: May 31
- Spring application deadline: December 5

## Tuition Model

HU publishes graduate tuition as a per-credit rate.

- Billing basis: `PER_CREDIT`
- Published rate: `USD 455` per credit hour
- Coverage: 3 of 3 in-scope graduate programs

## Tuition Mapping Result

- MA Education: mapped

No program-level tuition remains null.

## Scholarships / Financial Aid / Payment Summary

- HU publishes need-based financial aid and academic scholarships.
- Full-time graduate students may apply for financial aid, subject to limited availability.
- Tuition and fees are due at registration and at the beginning of each term.
- Installment payments are allowed through HU's signed request/declaration process.
- The catalog also publishes application, registration, and reservation fees.

## Remaining Official Gaps

- No university-wide graduate accreditation statement was isolated beyond the faculty-level IACBE accreditation for Business Administration and Economics.
