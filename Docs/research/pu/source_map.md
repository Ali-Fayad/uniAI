### Graduate admissions and required documents
Graduate admissions evidence exists for:

- MBA: online MBA application form, ID/passport, passport photo, bachelor degree equivalency, official bachelor transcripts/GPA, Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent, recommendation letter, updated resume, professional work experience proof, English proficiency evidence if prior university was not English-instruction, interview, application fee.
- LL.M.: online LL.M. application form, ID/passport, passport photo, law bachelor degree and equivalency, official bachelor transcripts/GPA, Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent, recommendation letter, updated resume, English proficiency evidence if prior university was not English-instruction, interview, application fee.

### Tuition / fees
Graduate tuition is publicly available from official sources:

- Master of Business Administration: $210 per credit.
- Master of Laws (LL.M.): $200 per credit.
- Graduate tuition page states tuition is paid per semester in fresh dollars.
- Graduate admissions pages list a non-refundable application fee of 1,000,000 LBP.
