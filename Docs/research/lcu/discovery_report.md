### Research Master of Business Administration

Official description: the Research MBA provides advanced theoretical knowledge and research competencies in business and management. It includes areas such as strategic management, finance, marketing, organizational behavior, economics, quantitative research methods, research methodology, statistical analysis, and academic writing.

Curriculum evidence found:

- Major requirements: 18 credits
- General requirements: 12 credits, including Research Methods in Business and Thesis
- Elective requirements: 6 credits
- Total from faculty listing: 36 credits
- Duration from faculty listing: 2 years

Important scope note: the page says Research MBA graduates are well-prepared to pursue doctoral studies / PhD in business and related disciplines. This is only an outcome statement and does not prove that LCU offers a PhD program.

### Executive Master of Business Administration

Official description: the Executive MBA is designed for working professionals and executives who want to strengthen leadership and managerial skills while continuing their careers. It focuses on strategic management, finance, marketing, leadership, operations, business analytics, practical application, and case-based learning.

- Requirements for non-business majors: remedial courses

- Total from faculty listing: 39 credits

## Admissions and required documents

The official Apply Now page provides required admission documents and application procedure, but the accessible text is written for undergraduate/freshman applicants. No graduate-specific admissions requirements were found on the accessible official pages.

General required documents visible on the official Apply Now page include:

- 3 recent passport-size photos
- Recent personal Civil Status Record or National Identity Card copy
- Passport and residence permit copy
- Recent Family Civil Status Record
- NSSF attestation or equivalent, if applicable
- Recent certificate of employment for the student or guarantor specifying monthly salary
- Official transcripts for the last three years of secondary school, certified by the issuing school
- Certified Lebanese Baccalaureate copy or equivalent issued by the Lebanese Ministry of Higher Education
- Official transcript of prior university study for transfer students

Because these requirements are presented in an undergraduate/freshman context, they should not be imported as definitive graduate admission requirements without additional official graduate-specific confirmation.
