## Admissions and required documents

Primary source: `MEU-S008`.

Graduate admission/application findings:

- Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree or equivalent from an accredited institution recognized by MEHE.
- Applicants must present applicable undergraduate GPA in the major area of study; the catalog section states 3.0 in the general eligibility list, then later defines regular graduate status as minimum undergraduate cumulative GPA 2.75 in the major area.
- Program-specific requirements may also apply under each degree program.
- Documents should be submitted at least one month before the beginning of the semester.
- Required graduate documents include:
  - Completed application form.
  - Recent digital passport-style photograph.
  - Authenticated copy of bachelor's diploma and official undergraduate transcript(s) sent directly to MEU.
  - Certified copy of baccalaureate certificate or equivalent.
  - English proficiency certificate if required, not older than two years.
  - Non-refundable application fee.
  - Passport main page for international applicants.
  - National identity card and family civil status record for Lebanese applicants.
  - Transfer applicants: official transcript in sealed envelope.

Application procedure page (`MEU-S007`) confirms the application fee context and online application process. Search result text showed $75 for graduate applicants; verify exact fee during import if using structured fee data.

## Language requirements

Primary source: `MEU-S008`.

Graduate applicants must demonstrate English proficiency and ability to research/write in English if they did not study their undergraduate program in English.

Accepted scores listed for graduate applicants:

- TOEFL ITP: minimum 600.
- TOEFL iBT: minimum 100.
- IELTS: minimum 7.0.
- Duolingo: minimum 120.

English exam scores are valid for up to two years. Departments offering MA, MAT, and MS may require GRE; MBA applicants may be required to sit for GMAT.

Additional program-specific language note from `MEU-S012`:

- MA Islamic Studies students whose mother tongue is not Arabic, or who did not complete secondary education in Arabic, must take two prerequisite 4-credit Arabic language courses outside the MA program.

## International students

Primary sources: `MEU-S008`, `MEU-S010`, `MEU-S013`, `MEU-S014`.

Findings:

- International applicants should allow time for application and visa processing.
- International applicants must pay an advance deposit and present a satisfactory budget for financing studies/living costs.
- Foreign document authentication requirements from MEHE apply.
- Applicants should not arrive physically in Lebanon before receiving official acceptance and travel authorization from MEU.
- Passport should be valid for at least one year from the date of joining MEU.
- MEU assists registered international students with residence permits.
- International graduate students are expected to carry a full course of study: minimum 8 credits per semester and 3 credits in summer to maintain international status.
- Tuition PDF snippets show foreign students must pay a $2,500 USD deposit credited to tuition/fees.

## Tuition / fees / payment plans

Primary sources: `MEU-S010`, `MEU-S013`, `MEU-S014`.

Findings:

- MEU expects tuition and fees to be paid in full during registration or alternative arrangements to be made with the Business Office.

## Scholarships / financial aid

Primary sources: `MEU-S011`, `MEU-S010`.

MEU offers financial aid/scholarship programs. Graduate-relevant items found:

- Needs-Based Financial Aid applies to tuition only for Lebanese undergraduate and graduate students, up to 40% financial aid on tuition only.
- Merit Tuition Scholarship includes entries for new graduate students and current graduate students.
- Work-Study Program includes graduate eligibility with minimum 3 credits per semester unless graduating.
- Family Discount applies to tuition paid only, with minimum-credit conditions.

Important rules from the Financial Aid page:

- Applications must be complete; missing information/documents make applications incomplete.
- Needs-Based Financial Aid applications are valid for one academic year only.
- Other aid may be granted per semester or under specific category rules.
- Aid and scholarships are generally not cumulative; higher aid applies at registration, with exceptions for Family Discount and Work Study.
- Andrews University program fee is excluded from MEU Financial Aid.
- Funds are limited and MEU may verify submitted documents.

## Graduate regulations

Primary source: `MEU-S008`.

Key graduate rules discovered:

- Admission valid for one calendar year from date of acceptance.
- Regular graduate status requires an earned bachelor's degree and minimum undergraduate cumulative GPA of 2.75 in the major area of study; regular status maintained with cumulative GPA 3.00 or above in graduate coursework.
- Full-time regular graduate load: 8-12 credits per semester; 7 credits or less is part-time; summer max 6 credits; above 12 credits requires approval; graduate students may not take more than 15 credits per semester.
- Probationary graduate status can be considered for applicants with minimum undergraduate GPA 2.50 in major area; they must complete first four graduate courses without repeats and earn minimum cumulative GPA 3.00.
- Applicants below 2.50 undergraduate GPA in major area are not admitted to the degree program.
- Provisional graduate status applies where foundation courses/deficiencies must be completed.
- Graduate transfer: max 20% of coursework from last four years with minimum 3.00 GPA or B equivalent.
- Graduate degree requirements must be completed within six academic years from beginning of first semester to thesis defense.
- Graduate specialized documents include MEU Standards for Written Work and Handbook for Graduate Students.
