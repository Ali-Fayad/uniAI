## Admissions Summary

- RHU graduate admissions are merit-based.
- Applicants submit the official graduate application and required documents.
- The reviewed official sources require proof of prior degree recognition/equivalence and English competency.
- The application process includes a non-refundable admission file fee.

## Required Documents Summary

- Certified copy of undergraduate degree and equivalence from the Ministry of Higher Education
- Official transcripts attested by the Ministry of Higher Education
- Certified copy of Lebanese Official High School Certificate or equivalent
- RHU English Entrance Exam or standardized English competency exam
- Recommendation letters
- Additional documents listed in the Graduate Catalog and application package

## Scholarship Summary

- RHU publishes financial-aid and discount language through the Financial Aid Office.
- The Graduate Catalog also describes limited merit-based graduate assistantships.
- No separate graduate scholarship table was recovered in this pass.

## Financial Aid Summary

- Need-based or discount-based financial aid is available through the Financial Aid Office.
- Limited merit-based graduate assistantships are described in the Graduate Catalog.
- Aid remains subject to academic and registration conditions.

## Payment Summary

- Students pay 25% of tuition plus other fees at the beginning of each semester.
- Deferred payment is available by signed contract.
- Installments are payable through the Finance Office process.
- Payment plans are structured as four installments for Fall and Spring and two installments for Summer.
