# LIU Discovery Report

Task code: LIU_DISCOVERY_001_BROWSER_INV  
Institution: Lebanese International University (LIU)  
Official website: https://www.liu.edu.lb  
Date accessed: 2026-07-04  
Mode: Browser-based discovery only

## Verdict

**APPROVE WITH NOTES**

LIU has sufficient official evidence to proceed to a structured extraction/import phase for master's programs. PhD evidence exists mainly as co-supervised/partner research activity, not as a clearly cataloged standalone LIU PhD degree offering. PharmD is documented as a doctoral degree but is outside the requested MASTER/PHD scope unless explicitly included later.

## Master's offered?

**YES**

Evidence found across official LIU pages and PDFs confirms graduate/master programs. The LIU Factsheet states LIU has five schools and 17 graduate majors, offering programs from bachelor degrees to masters to PharmD. The University Catalogue lists master programs by school, and several official plan-of-study PDFs are available for individual master programs.

## PhD offered?

**NO for standalone LIU PhD degree catalog evidence; YES for PhD co-supervision/research partnerships.**

The official Research & Graduate Studies page describes PhD students co-supervised by LIU faculty in partnerships with universities abroad, especially in Engineering. The Exchange Guide says LIU organizes Masters and PhD research programs. However, this pass did not find a standalone LIU PhD degree admissions/catalog section comparable to the master program sections. The official catalogue uses “Doctoral Degrees (PharmD),” which appears to refer to Doctor of Pharmacy rather than a PhD.

## Admissions / required documents

Master applicant requirements were found on the SAS graduate pages. Required documents include:

- Certified copy of Lebanese Baccalaureate / Technical Baccalaureate / equivalent foreign baccalaureate
- Certified copy of university degree or transcript, depending on page variant
- Identification card copy
- Family civil status record copy
- Recent passport-sized colored photograph
- National Social Security papers if available, or payment/form processing
- Graduate application fee
- English entrance/exam fee

General admissions page confirms students submit an online application or apply directly through admissions, and that the admission file must be completed with required documents.

## Tuition / fees / financial aid / payment plans

Official tuition page findings:

- Tuition is calculated per credit.
- Fees may change without notice.
- Students can pay through FNB and OMT.
- Financial aid is available generally, but School of Pharmacy students are not eligible for financial aid.
- Graduate tuition examples found:
  - School of Business graduate: USD 225 / credit
  - School of Engineering graduate: USD 285 / credit
  - MS Applied Math: USD 190 / credit
  - MS Food Technology: USD 240 / credit
  - Computer Science graduate: USD 285 / credit
  - School of Education graduate: USD 190 / credit
- Graduate Admission Application fee: LBP 2,500,000
- Registration Fee: USD 150
- Refund policy: 100% tuition refund during drop/add period; no tuition refund after add/drop except petition; registration fees are non-refundable.

Official brochure findings:

- Financial aid depends on academic achievement and financial need.
- Tuition payment can be divided into four payments per semester or eight payments per year.
