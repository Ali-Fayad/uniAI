4. Opened graduate program entry pages from the Degree Programs list.
5. Opened `Admissions`, `Application Process`, `Admission Requirements -> Graduate`, and `Important Dates`.
6. Opened `Office of Finance -> Tuition Fees` and `Yearly Schedule`.
7. Opened `Scholarship and Financial Aid` and `Graduate Tuition Reduction`.
