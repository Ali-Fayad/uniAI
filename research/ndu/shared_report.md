# NDU Shared Graduate Data Report

Date accessed: 2026-06-30

## Shared Admissions Summary

- Graduate applications are submitted online through SIS.
- The application fee is USD 30 and can be paid online or on campus.
- Graduate admission eligibility requires a recognized bachelor's degree or equivalent, a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.7/4.0 or equivalent, and the Lebanese Baccalaureate Part II or equivalent.
- NDU graduates are exempt from the English Entrance Test.
- Other applicants must submit TOEFL, SAT, or IELTS scores, or sit for the NDU EET.
- Program-specific exceptions exist for MBA and MS in Engineering applicants.

## Required Documents Summary

- Certified bachelor's degree copy or equivalent.
- Official undergraduate transcript.
- Certified Lebanese Baccalaureate Part II copy or equivalent.
- National ID card copy for Lebanese applicants or passport copy for international applicants.
- One recent passport-sized photograph.
- Two recommendation letters, one academic and one professional.
- NDU graduates have limited document exemptions on certified copies.

## English / Language Requirements

- Graduate applicants other than NDU graduates must submit TOEFL, SAT, or IELTS scores or take the NDU EET.
- No separate university-wide graduate language-of-instruction policy was published.
- The official graduate pages frame English proficiency as an admissions requirement rather than a program-by-program instruction policy.

## Deadline Summary

- Fall 2026 regular admission results and examination dates are posted on the Important Dates page.
- The current admissions page publishes Fall 2026 and Spring 2027 application timelines.
- The academic calendar and yearly schedule publish the 2025-2026 payment and registration milestones.
- Financial aid application periods are also published on the official site.

## Tuition Model

- NDU publishes a 2025-2026 graduate tuition table using per-credit billing.
- Category rates are Architecture USD 670, Business USD 635, Engineering USD 765, Computer Sciences USD 670, and All Others USD 590.
- A first payment of USD 1050 and a technology/student services fee are also published.
- Credit fees must be paid in full in cash USD.

## Scholarships, Financial Aid, and Payment Summary

- NDU offers a 25% graduate tuition reduction for eligible NDU graduates who enroll within two consecutive regular semesters after finishing the bachelor's degree.
- NDU also publishes a Fall 2026 Work Study Grant notice and a financial-aid application window.
- Tuition installments are published on the yearly schedule.
- Tuition and student services fees are paid in cash USD alongside each installment.
- The public pages do not expose a standalone graduate assistantship policy in a single central location.
