## Admissions Summary

- LGU's graduate studies page lists the baseline admission rules for graduate applicants.
- Applications are submitted online.
- Applicants upload supporting documents, pay the application fee, and complete an interview if required.
- General graduate admission criteria include a bachelor's degree from an accredited institution, a minimum GPA of 3.0 or equivalent, letters of recommendation, and a statement of purpose.
- GRE or GMAT may be required depending on the program.

## Required Documents Summary

- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
