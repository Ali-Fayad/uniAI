Date accessed: 2026-07-05  
Scope: MASTER and PHD only  
Discovery only: no JSON program import, no university JSON, no migrations, no seed files.

## Executive summary

A browser-based discovery pass was completed using only official LGU pages and official LGU-hosted PDFs/downloads.

The official Graduate Studies page is the only official page found that names Master's and Doctoral programs. It lists the following graduate program categories/names:

- Master's Programs:
  - Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  - Master of Science in Engineering
  - Master of Arts in Education
  - Master of Public Health
- Doctoral Programs:
  - Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in various disciplines
  - Doctor of Education (Ed.D.)
  - Doctor of Business Administration (DBA)

However, LGU's public Academic Programs catalog page showed 11 of 11 programs and all listed programs were Bachelor-level. No detailed official graduate program pages, graduate curricula, credit requirements, tuition tables, degree plans, or graduate catalog PDFs were found during this discovery pass.

Because of that mismatch, the graduate program names above should be treated as official but low-detail discovery evidence, not sufficient structured program records without a follow-up verification step.

Important limitation: no official dedicated PhD/doctoral detail pages, specializations, curriculum, credit requirements, dissertation rules, or admission pages were found during discovery. The public Academic Programs catalog page showed only Bachelor programs. Source: LGU-SRC-004.

### Admissions

The admissions landing page states that the online application process covers undergraduate and graduate programs and links to Graduate Studies. Source: LGU-SRC-002.

The Graduate Studies page lists general requirements:

- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
- Minimum GPA of 3.0 or equivalent
- Letters of recommendation
- Statement of purpose
- GRE or GMAT scores depending on program

It also lists the application process:

1. Submit online application
2. Upload required documents
3. Pay application fee
4. Schedule interview if required
5. Receive admission decision

Source: LGU-SRC-003.

### Required documents

The Financial Aid V7 official download lists required documents for financial aid. It includes a graduate/transfer-student-specific line:

- Copy of university transcript and tuition certificate for graduate and transfer students only

It also lists family register, high school/Bacc II grades for new applicants, sibling tuition certificates, employment certificates, tax/retirement/rental/car/medical/coverage documents where applicable.

Source: LGU-SRC-014.

The FAQ page contains admissions/document questions, including required documents and international student registration documents, but the accessible page text was mostly a question list rather than detailed answers. Source: LGU-SRC-009.

### Tuition / fees

No graduate tuition table or graduate cost-per-credit table was publicly available from official sources during this discovery pass.

The Undergraduate Studies page provides undergraduate cost-per-credit and administrative fees, but those values are out of scope and must not be reused for graduate programs. Source: LGU-SRC-006.

Graduate tuition was not publicly available from official sources.
