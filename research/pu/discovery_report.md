## Graduate admissions and required documents

### MBA admissions evidence

Official MBA graduate admissions requirements include:

- Completion of the online MBA admission application form
- Photocopy of identity card or passport
- One passport-sized photo
- Equivalency of the applicant's Bachelor's Degree from the Lebanese Ministry of Education and Higher Education
- Official transcripts from the university where the applicant completed the bachelor's degree, including overall GPA
- Official certificate for the Lebanese Baccalaureate or its recognized equivalent
- Recommendation letter from a professor or employer
- Updated resume
- Proof of professional work experience
- English proficiency evidence for applicants from universities where English is not the language of instruction, via IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo, or the Graduate PU English Placement Test
- Interview with the Admissions Committee
- Non-refundable application fee of 1,000,000 LBP

### LL.M. admissions evidence

Official LL.M. graduate admissions requirements include:

- Completion of the online Master of Laws (LLM) admission application form
- Photocopy of identity card or passport
- One passport-sized photo
- Bachelor's degree in Law from a recognized university accredited by the Lebanese Ministry of Education, with official equivalency
- Official transcripts from the university where the applicant completed the bachelor's degree, including overall GPA
- Official certificate for the Lebanese Baccalaureate or its recognized equivalent
- Recommendation letter from a professor or employer
- Updated resume
- English proficiency evidence for applicants from universities where English is not the language of instruction, via IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo English Test, or the Graduate PU English Placement Test
- Interview with the Admissions Committee
- Non-refundable application fee of 1,000,000 LBP

## Language requirements

Official graduate English language evidence found:

- MBA page: applicants from universities where English is not the language of instruction must provide evidence of English proficiency through IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo, or the Graduate PU English Placement Test.
- LL.M. page: applicants may satisfy graduate English language requirements through the PU Graduate English Language Placement Test or internationally recognized English proficiency examinations such as IELTS, TOEFL, or Duolingo.
- LL.M. page lists thresholds:
  - Meets requirement: Duolingo 135 or higher, TOEFL IBT 111 or higher, IELTS Academic 7 or higher.
  - Does not meet requirement: Duolingo less than 110, TOEFL IBT less than 90, IELTS Academic less than 6.5.
