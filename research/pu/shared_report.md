# PU Shared Report

## Shared Admissions Summary

- PU publishes separate graduate admissions pages for MBA and LL.M.
- Both programs use an online application, committee review, and interview step.
- The admissions pages also publish a non-refundable application fee.

## Required Documents Summary

- The MBA and LL.M. pages list overlapping core documents:
  - ID or passport
  - passport photo
  - official transcripts
  - Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent
  - recommendation letter
  - updated resume
- MBA applicants also need proof of professional work experience and bachelor's equivalency.
- LL.M. applicants need law-specific bachelor's credentials and equivalency.

## Language Requirement Summary

- Both programs require English-proficiency evidence when prior studies were not in English.
- The LL.M. page publishes explicit threshold values for Duolingo, TOEFL, and IELTS.

## Tuition Model

- PU publishes per-credit graduate tuition.
- MBA: $210 per credit.
- LL.M.: $200 per credit.
- Tuition is paid per semester in fresh dollars.
