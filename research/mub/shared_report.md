Date accessed: 2026-07-06

## Admissions Summary

- Graduate applications are handled through the official Apply Online form and the Faculty of Islamic Studies page.
- The Faculty of Islamic Studies requires a bachelor's degree, acceptable oration/communication skills, a personal statement, and a personal interview for master’s admission.
- Doctoral applicants must hold a master's degree in Islamic studies or equivalent with at least a good grade.

## Required Documents Summary

- The public application form explicitly requests ID card or passport information.
- The Faculty of Islamic Studies explicitly requires proof of a bachelor's degree for the master’s program.
- The Faculty of Islamic Studies explicitly requires a personal statement.

- No separate graduate document checklist was published.
