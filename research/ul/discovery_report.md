### PHD

Confirmed from official UL doctoral school pages and application announcements.

Doctoral schools discovered:

1. Doctoral School of Literature, Humanities & Social Sciences
   - Grants Lebanese Doctoral Degree and HDR.
   - Covered specializations include literature/humanities/social sciences, journalism/information, pedagogy, arts and art sciences, translation and language/communication sciences.

2. Doctoral School of Law, Political, Administrative & Economic Sciences
   - Prepares students for Lebanese PhD.
   - Provides grants for PhD students according to UL regulations.
   - Application announcements found for law/political/economic/administrative fields and business/economics/tourism.

3. Doctoral School of Science & Technology
   - Prepares students for Lebanese PhD in science and technology.
   - Covers engineering, public health, pharmacy, science, technology, medical sciences, dental medicine, fine arts/architecture and agronomy.
   - Internal system page states PhD equals 180 ECTS, including research/dissertation allocation.
