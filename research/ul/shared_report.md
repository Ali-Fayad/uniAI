## Shared Admissions Summary

- Graduate enrollment is handled by the relevant faculty or institute administration.
- Deadlines are set annually by each faculty or institute.
- Enrollment files are submitted with supporting documents.
- In-person attendance is required for enrollment unless a non-Lebanese applicant uses an authorized POA path.
- Some faculties publish online-admission workflows.

## Required Documents Summary

- Certified Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent
- Equivalency proof for non-Lebanese applicants
- Civil-status record or ID copy
- Passport copy for foreign students
- Two ID photos
- Social Security proof when applicable
- Postal stamp of LBP 1,000
- CEE registration fee of LBP 35,000 when applicable
- Certified transfer documents when a faculty accepts transfers
