# ALBA Source Map

Task code: `ALBA_DISCOVERY_001_BROWSER_INV`

Official website: <https://www.alba.edu.lb>

Discovery date: 2026-07-07

## Scope

In scope:
- Official Master's program pages
- Official PhD program pages
- Official graduate admissions pages
- Official graduate PDFs/catalogs
- Official tuition/fees, scholarship, regulations, and student guidance pages/PDFs

Out of scope:
- Bachelor/licence-only pages unless needed to document an inspected school
- Foundation year
- Diplomas/certificates unless clearly official Master's-level degrees
- Workshops, continuing education, professional training
- News, events, exhibitions, alumni, partnerships, faculty profiles, portfolio pages

## Discovery Notes

- The official site is SharePoint-based and some direct page fetches timed out or returned gateway errors during the browser pass. Search-index snippets and the active page context were used only when they came from official `alba.edu.lb` URLs.
- Primary Master evidence was accepted only from official ALBA pages or official ALBA PDFs.
- PhD references found in job posts, researcher bios, alumni descriptions, or doctor/doctoral status references were excluded because they do not prove an official ALBA PhD program.
