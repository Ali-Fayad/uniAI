## Search and inspection notes

- Official current catalog was treated as the strongest source because it is a current AUT PDF and explicitly lists graduate programs, admissions, tuition/fees, aid, and international student notes.
- Search results containing careers/job postings were excluded as program evidence, even where they mention graduate teaching or PhD qualifications.
- Search results containing news/events, ceremonies, and partnerships were excluded as primary program evidence.
- External University of London pages were not added to `sources.json` because validation requires official AUOT URLs only. The AUT LLM page was kept as an official AUT support-program source.
