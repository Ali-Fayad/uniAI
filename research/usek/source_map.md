# USEK Graduate Source Map

## Scope

This is a discovery-only map for Holy Spirit University of Kaslik (USEK).
Current in-scope graduate degree types for the project:
- MASTER
- PHD

Out of scope for later inventory:
- certificates
- diplomas
- undergraduate degrees
- professional training
- continuing education

## Graduate Scope Confirmation

- USEK clearly offers master’s programs.
- USEK clearly offers doctorate / PhD programs.
- Doctoral studies are also organized through a dedicated Doctoral College.
- The site exposes both graduate admissions and graduate fees pages.

## Missing Source Categories

- A single centralized graduate handbook was not found.
- A standalone graduate-only scholarship/assistantship hub was not found.
- A dedicated graduate tuition PDF was not found; tuition is published on the graduate-fees page instead.
- A clean English graduate catalog landing page was not found; several resources are English but the site remains mixed-language.
- Some graduate program pages are nested deeply under faculty or department URLs, which will require careful normalization later.

## Next-Phase Risks

- USEK uses a mix of hub pages, faculty pages, and nested program pages.
- Out-of-scope diploma pages appear alongside graduate pages and must be filtered carefully.
- Some graduate tuition data is present in a central fee table, but some future program-specific exceptions may still need manual review.
- Doctoral content is split between Doctoral College and admissions pages, so PhD discovery needs both sources.
