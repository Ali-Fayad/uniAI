## Admissions Data Found
- Undergraduate degree or equivalent recognized by the Lebanese Ministry of Education and Higher Education.
- Program/unit Graduate Admission Interview Committee recommendation.
- Program-specific requirements when applicable.
- Online graduate application with required documents.
- Non-refundable USD 30 admission file fee.
- Doctoral applicants: master's degree or equivalent, and program-eligible doctoral path / proposal options.

## Required Documents Found
- General graduate application documents listed in the admissions guide.
- Doctoral-specific document checklist published by the Doctoral Studies page.

## Scholarships / Aid Found
- Financial aid and discounts through the Financial Aid Office.
- Agreement-based reductions.
- Scholarship-by-admission language is published, but it is not a separate graduate-only table.

## Payment Plan Data Found
- Four installments per semester (Fall and Spring).
- Two installments for Summer Session.
- First payment: USD 1000 including the USD 200 registration fee.
- Registration fee: USD 200 per semester.
- Operational fee: USD 300 per semester.
- NSSF membership fee: USD 94 per academic year.
