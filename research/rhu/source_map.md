## Source strategy

The 2025-2026 Graduate Catalog is the strongest source because it consolidates graduate programs, admissions, academic calendar, application package, payment methods, graduate regulations, graduate assistantship, financial aid, MBA requirements, and engineering MS requirements.

## Discovery notes by topic

### Programs

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-003, RHU-SRC-011, RHU-SRC-013, RHU-SRC-014, RHU-SRC-015, RHU-SRC-016, RHU-SRC-017.

In-scope graduate programs discovered:

- MBA - General Track / General Business Management emphasis.
- MBA - Oil and Gas Management emphasis.
- MS in Civil and Environmental Engineering.
- MS in Biomedical Engineering.
- MS in Computer and Communications Engineering.
- MS in Electrical Engineering.
- MS in Mechanical Engineering.
- MS in Mechatronics Engineering.

No official RHU PhD / Doctorate program was found in the inspected graduate catalog, graduate programs page, or college pages.

### Admissions and required documents

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-004, RHU-SRC-005, RHU-SRC-006, RHU-SRC-011.

Graduate admissions are merit-based. Required application package items include certified undergraduate degree/equivalence, official transcripts, Lebanese Official High School Certificate or equivalent, English entrance exam or standardized English competency exam, recommendation letters, and other documents listed in the catalog/application package.

### Tuition, fees, payment, payment plans

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-007, RHU-SRC-008, RHU-SRC-009, RHU-SRC-011.

The current 2025-2026 fee PDF lists graduate per-credit tuition by college/level. It also lists enrollment, deposit, NSSF, service fees, deferred payment, late registration/payment, transportation/parking, and dormitory/housing fees. Deferred payment is listed as scheduled over four installments for fall/spring and two installments for summer.

### Academic calendar and regulations

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-011, RHU-SRC-018.

The Graduate Catalog 2025-2026 includes the academic calendar and graduate academic regulations, including period of study, orientation, supervision, courses and grades, course load, plan of study, academic standing, graduate assistantship, and graduation requirements.

### Scholarships, financial aid, assistantship

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-005, RHU-SRC-011.

The Graduate Catalog includes a Financial Aid Program section and a Graduate Assistantship section. RHU offers limited merit-based graduate assistantships every term excluding summer.

### International students, passport/visa, housing

Primary sources: RHU-SRC-005, RHU-SRC-011.

The Graduate Catalog includes a Passport and Visa section. The Before Applying page and fee PDF include housing/dormitory information and fees.

## Out-of-scope items deliberately ignored

- Undergraduate catalogs and undergraduate program pages.
- Bachelor/BBA/BE/BS pages except where unavoidable in navigation context.
- News and events shown on home/college pages.
- Alumni pages, campaign pages, and media pages.
- Non-degree admissions and continuing/professional content.
