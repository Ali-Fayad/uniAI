## Tuition Table Extracted

- 33 in-scope master’s programs were assigned official 2026-2027 tuition values.
- All tuition values are stored in the program records as structured objects.
- Billing basis is PER_CREDIT for every in-scope LAU graduate program.
- Online master’s offerings use the official LAU online master’s tuition of 580 USD per credit.

## Fee Table Extracted

- Graduate application fee: 50 USD one-time.

## Admissions Requirements Found

- Selective admission to a recognized bachelor’s degree holder with academic ability and motivation.
- Required documents: photo, ID/passport, civil status record where applicable, certified certificates/diplomas, transcripts, degree equivalency where applicable, secondary diploma equivalency where applicable, two recommendations, employment certificate where applicable, and W-9S for eligible U.S. applicants.
- English requirements: TOEFL iBT 90, IELTS 7, EEE 123, or Duolingo 125; conditional remediation thresholds are also specified.
- Shared deadlines: late admission for Fall 2026, late graduate assistantship, and graduate research scholarship deadline.
- No universal GRE or GMAT requirement was found.

## Financial Aid / Scholarship Information Found

- Graduate assistantships cover full or partial tuition and are available only for in-person programs.
- Graduate Research Scholarship provides full tuition waiver for two years for a master’s degree.
- Grants, loans, and Tomorrow’s Leaders Graduate (TLG) support are listed on the graduate aid page.
