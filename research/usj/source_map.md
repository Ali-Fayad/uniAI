- Certificates
- Diplomas
- Executive education
- Continuing education

## Program Pages / Program Hub Observations

### Graduate catalog hub
The French graduate catalog hub is the primary discovery surface. It lists many master's programs and also exposes out-of-scope offerings such as diplomas and certificates.

Observed in-scope master’s titles include:
- MBA / business management offerings
- Master of Science offerings in sciences and engineering
- Master of Arts offerings in humanities, education, translation, international affairs, and journalism
- Law-adjacent graduate offerings

### Doctoral surface
The Faculty of Sciences graduate page exposes doctoral program links. The discovery pass confirmed that USJ has doctoral study, but a single university-wide PhD index page was not located.

### PDF program surfaces
Official PDFs were also discovered on faculty pages and are useful later when a program page is published primarily as a PDF rather than HTML.

### French-only or French-first resources
Most official graduate resources are French-first.
- The graduate catalog hub is French.
- Many faculty pages are French-only.
- The master admissions page is French.
- Some English navigation exists, but the graduate detail surfaces are overwhelmingly French.
