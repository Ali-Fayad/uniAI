## Discovery notes

- The official program inventory describes “20 nationally ranked undergraduate programs” and lists programs under Arts and Sciences, Education, and Management/Finance/Economics, plus Continuing Education and Diploma. No Master's or PhD program is listed.
- The FAQ directly addresses postgraduate study programs and says USAL has requested licensing for some postgraduate programs and is awaiting ministerial decisions before offering them. This is treated as evidence that no current official postgraduate offering is publicly available.
- Tuition, admissions, application, and financial aid pages were inspected for graduate-specific language. No graduate tuition, graduate admission route, graduate application form, or graduate-specific aid was found.
- Official PDFs found and inspected in scope were academic regulations and program/recognition PDFs. They did not provide evidence of current Master's or PhD offerings.

## Out-of-scope evidence intentionally excluded

- Bachelor programs and concentrations.

- Faculty biographies mentioning faculty members' own Master's or PhD degrees.
- Job postings requiring a PhD.
- News/events about USAL graduates pursuing graduate studies abroad.
- Research activity, research centers, publications, or partnerships.
