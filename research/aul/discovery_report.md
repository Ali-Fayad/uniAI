## Admissions and Required Documents

The official Admissions page states that AUL applicants shall fill the Admission Application Form at the campus of registration and provide documents required by assigned deadlines.

Documents listed by AUL include:

- Admission Application Form (4 pages)
- Three recent passport-size colored photos
- Certified copy of Identity Card or Passport
- Certified copy of Family Register
- Certified copy of Official Lebanese BAC II Certificate or equivalent / freshman authorization with SAT
- Certified copy of high-school grades for the last three years
- Certified copies of degree(s) and official transcript(s) from previous university stamped by the Lebanese Ministry of Education & Higher Education, in addition to equivalence for graduate programs
- International testing scores and certificates, if available
- Medical report
- NSSF letter, if available
- Proof of residency letter

Official source: AUL-002

## Financial Aid / Scholarships / Assistantship

The official Admissions page states that an applicant must be a degree-seeking student to qualify for financial aid from AUL, and that applications are available at the Student Affairs department and should be completed before the university calendar deadline.

The same page includes visible labels for `SIBLINGS`, `SPORTS`, and `SCHOLARSHIPS`, but no detailed graduate-specific award values were publicly available from the official page content reviewed.

The official AUL-hosted Student Work Program Handbook states that graduate students in a master's program (MS or MBA) are eligible for the student work program / assistantship, subject to its criteria.

Official sources: AUL-002, AUL-009

## Tuition / Fees
