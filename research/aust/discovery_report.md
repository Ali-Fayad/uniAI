## Admissions and required documents

Sources: `AUST-SRC-003`, `AUST-SRC-004`, `AUST-SRC-005`.

Found:

- Official online graduate application portal.
- Graduate applicants must submit supporting documents including CV, identity/passport copy, passport photos, application fee, Lebanese family civil status record where applicable, certified educational/professional certificates, official transcript, and recommendations.
- Graduate admission decisions are made by the Graduate Committee according to academic qualifications and available places.
- Admission validity is one calendar year.
- Graduate admission page indicates eligible candidates should hold a bachelor's or equivalent degree, meet TOEFL/AUST English equivalent expectations, and submit a statement of purpose in English.

## Language requirements

Sources: `AUST-SRC-003`, `AUST-SRC-005`.

Found:

- Applicants should demonstrate English proficiency through prior English-language instruction or pass the English Entrance Exam / TOEFL equivalent path.
- Graduate application instructions mention EEE minimum score 550 or TOEFL minimum score 575/equivalent, with EEE 500-549 requiring an undergraduate English course.
- The graduate admission requirements page mentions TOEFL 600 or equivalent AUST English entrance exam score.

Note: There is a discrepancy between the TOEFL thresholds in two official AUST sources. Treat this as a required human-review item before seeding exact language-test thresholds.

## Tuition, fees, financial aid, and payment plans

Sources: `AUST-SRC-007`, `AUST-SRC-008`.

Found:

- Graduate tuition source lists MBA and MS graduate clear tuition at **USD 320 per credit hour**.
- Graduate-on-probation/remedial/prerequisite rates are listed separately.
- Other fees include application, registration, accident insurance, technology, social security, and selected laboratory fee information.
- Payment method: students must pay 25% of tuition fees plus other fees at the beginning of each semester; deferred/installment payment is available by signed contract and installments are payable at the bank.
- Financial aid page confirms partial scholarships for candidates with financial need who meet credit-load and GPA requirements.

## Academic rules and regulations

Sources: `AUST-SRC-006`, `AUST-SRC-021`, `AUST-SRC-022`.

Found:

- Master's degree completion requires 30 to 39 graduate credits depending on program.
- Minimum cumulative GPA requirement: 3.00 / 4.00.
- No more than two C grades are allowed during the graduate program study period.
- Rules and regulations PDF notes graduate students should carry a minimum of 6 credit hours to be considered full-time.
- Students Handbook PDF is an official broad policy source for admission, registration, tuition and fees, attendance, curriculum, conduct, academic standing, candidacy, and graduation.
