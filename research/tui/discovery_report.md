# Discovery Report — BROWSER_TUI_DISCOVERY_001

## Recommendation

**APPROVE WITH NOTES**

The official University of Tripoli / Tripoli University Institute website provides official evidence that the university currently offers graduate study at both Master's and PhD levels. A graduate inventory may be created, but it should be limited to the official programs/faculties supported by the captured sources.

In scope:

- MASTER
- PHD

Out of scope:

- Bachelor
- Diploma
- Certificate
- Continuing Education
- Professional Programs
- Training

- Events

- https://ut.edu.lb

Official Master's evidence found.

Official PhD evidence found.
